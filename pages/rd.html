Roasted Duck Dish & Recipe

Homepage

This is Roasted Duck, which is a must have at every Chinese dinner. Its delicious by giving you that sweet and salty flavor.

You will need the follow ingredients to make this:

  1. Duck
  2. Soy Sauce
  3. Dark Soy Sauce
  4. Hoisin Sauce
  5. Water
  6. Garlic
  7. Ginger
  8. Star Anise
  9. Clove
  10. Five Spices

First you will need to grind the garlic (2 cloves), ginger (3 slices), star anise (3 pcs), cloves (3 pcs), and five spices (1/8th tsp) together. Once grinded, mix the mixture in with soy sauce (1/2 cup), hoisin sauce (1/2 cup), dark soy sauce (2 tbs), and water (1 cup).

Wash and clean your duck. Put the duck into the sauce mixture you've made. You should allow it to marinate overnight for best results.

After letting it marinate overnight or however long, preheat over to 375 fahrenheit. Wrap Duck's wings with foil and roast duck in oven on a cake stand like mold or something similar to it.

After an hour of letting the duck bake in the oven, take the foil away from the wings and change the oven to 425 fahrenheit. Bake foe 15-20 minutes or until the duck reaches a 165 internal temperature and is golden brown.

Let it rest for at least 10 minutes and then enjoy to your liking!

NOTE: There's usually a sauce that you're supposed to eat with the Roasted Duck, but couldn't find it. However, I do think that the sauce is just the juices that is produced from baking the duck.
