Chinese Yellow Chicken

This is Green Ginger Scallion Oil, which is mainly eaten with Chinese Yellow Chicken. It's a delcious and flavorful sauce that honestly goes well with anything!

You will need the follow ingredients to make this:

  1. Ginger
  2. Scallions
  3. Oil
  4. Ginger
  5. Salt

Wash and dry scallions. Dice them up. You should get around 4 tbsp of scallions. Peel and slice ginger, then dice them up. You should get around 3 tbsp.

At this point you're done. All you have to do is mix-up the scallion, ginger, oil, and salt together by adjusting the oil and salt to your taste.

An optional thing to do, if you're eating Chinese Yellow Chicken, is that you can split the sauce into two different bowls and in one the bowls you can add light soy sauce to your liking. Enjoy!
