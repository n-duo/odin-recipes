Chinese Kayle (Gai lan) Dish & Recipe

Homepage

This is Chinese Kayle (Gai lan), which is one of the most delicious vegetables I've ever had! There are many ways to cook this vegetable. In this recipe I'll teach you one of easiest and delicious ways I like to enjoy it.

You will need the follow ingredients to make this:

  1. Chinese Kayle (Gai lan)
  2. Salt
  3. Garlic
  4. Oyster Sauce
  5. Dark Soy Sauce
  6. Sugar
  7. Cornstarch
  8. Water

Clean and chop the end stems of the Chinese Kayle (Gai lan). Boil a pot of water with salt and oil and put the Chinese Kayle (Gai lan) in. Boil for only around one minute.

During this time, you can prepare the sauce. Get a pan and start sauteing garlic with oil. When garlic starts to become brownish, pour oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, cornstarch and water into the pan to your liking. Generally, the sauce should look thick but still runny.

At this point your vegetables should be done. Quickly drain and transfer vegetables to a plat and pour the sauce over the vegetables.

THAT'S IT! You're done. Enjoy this vegetable with a bowl of steamed rice or even by itself!
